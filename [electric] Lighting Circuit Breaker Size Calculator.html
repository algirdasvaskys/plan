Calculating Lighting Circuit Breaker Size for Your Home

Determining the correct circuit breaker size for your home's lighting circuit is crucial for safety and proper electrical function. This guide will walk you through the process and provide a calculator to help you determine the right size.

Step-by-step Method

  1. Count the number of light fixtures on the circuit
  2. Determine the wattage of each fixture
  3. Calculate the total wattage by multiplying the number of fixtures by the average wattage per fixture
  4. Convert the total wattage to amperes by dividing by the voltage (typically 120V in the US)
  5. Choose a circuit breaker size that is larger than the calculated amperage, typically the next standard size up
