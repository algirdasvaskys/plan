The Big Build 26: Rafters, Ridges, and Walls

1. Rafters

Step 1: Measure and Cut

Measure the length needed for your rafters and cut the lumber accordingly. Remember to account for overhangs.

Step 2: Create Bird's Mouth Cut

Make a bird's mouth cut on each rafter where it will rest on the top plate of the wall.

Step 3: Install Rafters

Begin installing rafters at one end of the building, working your way to the other end. Secure each rafter to the ridge board and wall plate.

2. Ridges

Step 1: Install Ridge Board

Place the ridge board at the peak of the roof, supported temporarily by posts.

Step 2: Secure Ridge Board

As you install rafters, secure them to the ridge board using framing nails or hurricane ties.

3. Walls

Step 1: Frame the Wall

Build wall frames on the ground, including top and bottom plates and studs.

Step 2: Raise the Wall

Lift the framed wall into place and secure it to the floor and adjacent walls.

Step 3: Install Sheathing

Cover the exterior of the wall frame with sheathing material, typically plywood or OSB.

Safety Warning: Always wear appropriate safety gear and follow local building codes. If you're unsure about any part of the process, consult with a professional contractor.
