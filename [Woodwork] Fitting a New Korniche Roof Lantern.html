Fitting a New Korniche Roof Lantern

Warning: Fitting a roof lantern involves working at height and handling large, heavy materials. If you're not confident in your DIY skills or don't have experience with this type of work, it's recommended to hire a professional.

Tools and Materials Needed:

  • Korniche roof lantern kit
  • Drill with appropriate bits
  • Screwdriver
  • Spirit level
  • Tape measure
  • Silicone sealant and gun
  • Safety equipment (gloves, goggles, hard hat)
  • Ladder or scaffolding
  • Assistant (recommended)

Step-by-Step Installation Process:

Step 1: Prepare the Roof Opening

Ensure the roof opening is clean, dry, and structurally sound. The opening should be slightly larger than the external dimensions of the lantern to allow for fitting.

Step 2: Install the Timber Kerb

If not already in place, install a timber kerb around the roof opening. This should be level and securely fixed to the roof structure.

Step 3: Apply Weatherproofing

Apply a layer of weatherproof membrane or flashing to the timber kerb, ensuring it overlaps onto the roof to prevent water ingress.

Step 4: Position the Korniche Frame

Carefully lift the Korniche frame onto the prepared kerb. Check it's square and level, adjusting as necessary.

Step 5: Secure the Frame

Use the provided fixings to secure the frame to the kerb. Ensure all fixings are tight but be careful not to over-tighten.

Step 6: Install the Glazing

Carefully place the glazing units into the frame, starting with the larger sheets first. Secure using the provided glazing retainers and caps.

Step 7: Fit the Ridge and Hip Caps

Install the ridge cap along the top of the lantern and the hip caps along the corners, ensuring they're properly aligned and secure.

Step 8: Apply Sealant

Apply silicone sealant to all joints and any areas where water could potentially enter.

Step 9: Final Checks

Double-check all fixings are secure, the lantern is level, and all seals are in place. Clean any excess sealant or fingerprints from the glass and frame.

Important: Always follow the manufacturer's specific instructions provided with your Korniche roof lantern kit. These steps are a general guide and may vary slightly depending on your specific model and roof configuration.

Maintenance

To keep your Korniche roof lantern in good condition:

  • Clean the glass regularly with a mild detergent solution
  • Check and clean the drainage channels annually
  • Inspect seals and sealant annually, replacing if necessary
  • Lubricate any moving parts (if applicable) according to manufacturer's instructions
