Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Identify the components:
    • Voltmeter
    • 2 Position Selector Switch
    • Circuit to be measured
  2. Connect the negative (COM) terminal of the voltmeter to the common ground of the circuit.
  3. Connect the positive terminal of the voltmeter to the center terminal (pole) of the 2 position selector switch.
  4. Connect one throw of the selector switch to one point in the circuit where you want to measure voltage.
  5. Connect the other throw of the selector switch to another point in the circuit where you want to measure voltage.
  6. Ensure all connections are secure and properly insulated.
  7. Set the voltmeter to the appropriate range for your expected voltage measurements.
  8. Use the selector switch to choose between the two measurement points:
    • Position 1: Measures voltage at the first connection point
    • Position 2: Measures voltage at the second connection point
  9. Read the voltage displayed on the voltmeter for each switch position.
  10. Always turn off the power to the circuit before making any changes to the connections.

Safety Precautions

  • Always use a voltmeter with a sufficient voltage rating for your circuit.
