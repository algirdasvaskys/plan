Changing Motor Running Direction in Star Delta Starter

Warning: Working with electrical systems can be dangerous. Always ensure that the power is off and follow proper safety procedures before making any changes. If you're not qualified to work with electrical systems, please consult a professional electrician.

Introduction

In a three-phase induction motor with a Star Delta starter, changing the direction of rotation is a relatively simple process. It involves swapping any two of the three phase connections to the motor.

Method

To change the motor running direction in a Star Delta starter, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure the motor and starter are completely powered off and disconnected from the power source.
  2. Locate the motor terminal box.
  3. Identify the three main power leads connected to the motor (usually labeled U, V, and W or L1, L2, and L3).
  4. Choose any two of these leads to swap.
  5. Disconnect the chosen leads from their current terminals.
  6. Reconnect these leads to each other's previous terminals.
  7. Ensure all connections are tight and secure.
  8. Close the terminal box.
  9. Reconnect the power source.
  10. Test the motor to confirm the change in rotation direction.

Important Considerations

  • This method works for both Star (Y) and Delta (Î”) configurations.
  • The change in connections should be made at the motor terminals, not at the starter.
  • Swapping any two phases will reverse the motor's direction, regardless of which two you choose.
  • Ensure that changing the motor's direction won't cause any issues with the connected machinery or process.
  • If the motor has been running in one direction for a long time, it's advisable to check the bearings before running it in the opposite direction.

Safety Precautions

  • Always disconnect and lock out the power supply before working on the motor or starter.
  • Use appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE).
  • Verify that the motor is completely stopped before making any changes.
  • Double-check all connections before restoring power.
  • Ensure that changing the motor's direction won't create any safety hazards in your specific application.

By following these steps and considerations, you can safely change the running direction of a motor using a Star Delta starter. Remember, if you're unsure about any step in the process, it's always best to consult with a qualified electrician or the motor manufacturer.
