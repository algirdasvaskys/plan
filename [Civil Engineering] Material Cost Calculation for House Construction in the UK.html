Material Cost Calculation for House Construction in the UK

Note: This guide provides estimated material costs based on average UK prices as of 2024. Actual costs may vary based on location, market conditions, and specific project requirements.

1. Determine the Scope of the Project

For this example, we'll consider a typical 3-bedroom house with approximately 100 square meters (1,076 square feet) of floor space.

2. Sample Material List with UK Prices

Here's a breakdown of some common materials and their estimated costs:

Material Unit Price per Unit (£)
Concrete per m³ 90-110
Bricks per 1000 400-600
Timber (structural) per m³ 300-400
Roof tiles per m² 25-40
Insulation (fibreglass) per m² 5-8
Drywall per m² 8-12
Windows (double-glazed) per window 300-500
Interior doors per door 70-150

4. Additional Considerations

  • This calculation doesn't include all materials. You'll also need to account for plumbing, electrical, flooring, paint, and more.
  • Labour costs are not included and can significantly increase the total project cost.
  • Prices can vary based on quality, brand, and supplier. Always get multiple quotes.
  • Don't forget to factor in VAT (currently 20% in the UK) on materials.
Remember: This is a rough estimate based on average prices. For an accurate quote, consult with local suppliers and professional contractors. Prices may have changed since this estimate was created in 2024.
