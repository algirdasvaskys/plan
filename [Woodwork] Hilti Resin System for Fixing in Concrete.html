Hilti Resin System for Fixing in Concrete

This guide provides a simple and easy-to-use method for using Hilti's resin system to fix anchors in concrete.

Materials Needed:

  • Hilti resin capsule or cartridge
  • Hilti dispenser gun (for cartridge system)
  • Hilti anchor rod or threaded bar
  • Rotary hammer drill
  • Brush and blow-out pump
  • Personal protective equipment (safety glasses, gloves)

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Drill the Hole

Use a rotary hammer drill to create a hole in the concrete. Ensure the hole diameter and depth match the specifications for your chosen anchor.

Step 2: Clean the Hole

Clean the hole thoroughly using a brush and blow-out pump. Remove all dust and debris for optimal adhesion.

Step 3: Prepare the Resin

For capsule system: Insert the resin capsule into the hole.
For cartridge system: Load the resin cartridge into the dispenser gun.

Step 4: Insert the Anchor

For capsule system: Insert the anchor rod, rotating it to mix the resin components.
For cartridge system: Inject the resin into the hole, then insert the anchor rod.

Step 5: Allow Curing

Let the resin cure according to the manufacturer's specified time. This varies based on temperature and humidity.

Step 6: Apply Load

Once fully cured, the anchor is ready to bear the designed load.

Important Notes:

  • Always follow Hilti's specific product instructions and safety guidelines.
  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment.
  • Ensure proper ventilation when working with resin systems.
  • Dispose of unused resin and packaging according to local regulations.

Benefits of Hilti Resin System:

  • High load capacity
  • Suitable for cracked and non-cracked concrete
  • Fast curing times
  • Corrosion-resistant options available
