Diesel Generator Sizing Guide

1. Understanding Generator Capacity

Generator capacity is typically measured in kilowatts (kW) or kilovolt-amperes (kVA). The relationship between kW and kVA depends on the power factor:

  • Typical power factor for generators: 0.8

2. Load Calculation

To determine the appropriate generator size, you need to calculate the total load:

  1. List all electrical equipment that will be powered by the generator
  2. Note the power rating (in watts or kW) for each piece of equipment
  3. Consider starting loads for motors (typically 3 times the running load)
  4. Sum up all the loads

3. Sizing Considerations

When sizing a generator, consider the following factors:

  • Peak load requirements
  • Future load growth
  • Altitude and temperature derating
  • Continuous vs. standby operation
