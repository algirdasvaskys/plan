Making Window Reveal Strips in Cladding Series

Window reveal strips are an essential component in the cladding series, providing a clean finish and weather protection around window openings. This guide will walk you through the process of making and installing these strips.

Materials Needed:

  • Aluminum or PVC strips
  • Measuring tape
  • Pencil or marker
  • Tin snips or a fine-toothed saw
  • File or sandpaper
  • Sealant
  • Screws or nails
  • Drill or hammer

Step-by-Step Process:

Step 1: Measure the Window Opening

Carefully measure the height and width of the window opening. Add an extra 50mm to each measurement to allow for overlap and cutting adjustments.

Step 2: Cut the Reveal Strips

Using your measurements, mark and cut the reveal strips to size. For the vertical strips, cut two pieces to the height measurement. For the horizontal strips, cut two pieces to the width measurement.

Step 3: Miter the Corners

To create neat corners, miter the ends of the strips at a 45-degree angle. This will allow them to fit together seamlessly at the corners of the window.

Step 4: File the Edges

Use a file or sandpaper to smooth any rough edges on the cut strips. This will ensure a clean finish and prevent any potential injuries during handling.

Step 5: Apply Sealant

Apply a bead of weatherproof sealant along the back edge of each strip. This will create a watertight seal when the strips are installed.

Step 6: Install the Strips

Start with the bottom horizontal strip, then install the vertical strips, and finish with the top horizontal strip. Secure each strip in place using screws or nails, ensuring they are level and properly aligned.

Step 7: Seal the Joints

Apply additional sealant to the joints where the strips meet, as well as around the edges where the strips meet the window frame and the cladding.

Note:

Always ensure that the reveal strips are compatible with your cladding system and follow any specific manufacturer guidelines. Proper installation is crucial for effective weather protection and aesthetic appeal.

Conclusion

By following these steps, you can create and install window reveal strips that will enhance the appearance of your cladding while providing essential protection against the elements. Remember to take accurate measurements and work carefully for the best results.
