Feeling Is the Secret

Summary

"Feeling Is the Secret" by Neville Goddard is a self-help book that emphasizes the power of emotions and beliefs in shaping reality. Goddard argues that the subconscious mind is driven by feeling, which acts as the secret to manifesting desires. By aligning our feelings with our goals, we can influence our outer world and achieve personal success.

Themes

The Power of the Subconscious Mind

The book highlights the importance of the subconscious mind in creating one's reality. Goddard stresses that the subconscious is a powerful force that responds to emotions and beliefs, making it essential to cultivate positive feelings.

Feeling as a Creative Force

Goddard teaches that feeling is the creative force behind all experiences. By feeling the reality of the desired outcome, individuals can bring their dreams into existence.

Imagination and Reality

The book discusses the role of imagination in shaping reality. Goddard encourages using vivid imagination to feel the presence of the desired state, thereby influencing the subconscious to manifest it.

Alignment of Conscious and Subconscious

Achieving harmony between the conscious and subconscious mind is crucial. Goddard suggests that conscious thoughts must align with the feelings held in the subconscious to effectively manifest desires.
