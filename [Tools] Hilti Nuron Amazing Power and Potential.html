Hilti Nuron: Amazing Power and Potential

Hilti Nuron is a revolutionary battery platform that offers unprecedented power and versatility for construction professionals. This new system represents a significant leap forward in cordless tool technology.

Key Features of Hilti Nuron

  • High-performance lithium-ion battery technology
  • Cordless power rivaling corded tools
  • Extended runtime for increased productivity
  • Intelligent battery management system
  • Durability for tough jobsite conditions
  • Comprehensive range of compatible tools

Nuron Battery Platform

The Nuron battery platform is the heart of this innovative system. It offers:

  • 22V voltage for high power output
  • Multiple capacity options (2.0Ah to 12.0Ah)
  • Fast charging capabilities
  • Built-in battery health monitoring
  • Extreme weather performance (-20°C to 60°C)

New Hilti Nuron Tools

TE 60-22 Combihammer

A powerful cordless rotary hammer for heavy-duty drilling and chiseling in concrete.

  • Drilling range: 12-40 mm
  • Impact energy: 8.1 J
  • Active Vibration Reduction (AVR) for user comfort

AG 600-22 Angle Grinder

High-performance cordless angle grinder for cutting and grinding applications.

  • No-load speed: 10,000 rpm
  • Disc diameter: 150 mm
  • Brushless motor for extended life and runtime

SF 10W-22 Drill Driver

Versatile drill driver for a wide range of drilling and fastening tasks.

  • Max. torque: 110 Nm
  • 4-speed gearbox for optimal performance
  • Compact design for use in tight spaces
