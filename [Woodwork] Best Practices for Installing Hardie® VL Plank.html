Best Practices for Installing Hardie® VL Plank

Safety First: Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) including safety glasses, dust mask, and gloves when working with Hardie® products.

Materials and Tools Needed:

  • Hardie® VL Plank
  • Corrosion-resistant fasteners
  • Circular saw with diamond-tipped blade
  • Tape measure
  • Level
  • Chalk line
  • Caulk gun and sealant
  • Paint and paintbrushes (if finishing on-site)

Installation Steps:

  1. Prepare the Surface:

    Ensure the wall surface is flat, clean, and dry. Install weather-resistant barrier according to manufacturer's instructions.

  2. Plan the Layout:

    Measure and mark the starting point. Use a level and chalk line to ensure a straight installation.

  3. Install Starter Strip:

    Attach a starter strip at the bottom of the wall to provide a level base for the first row of planks.

  4. Cut Planks:

    Measure and cut planks as needed using a circular saw with a diamond-tipped blade. Always cut Hardie® products outdoors and wear a dust mask.

  5. Install First Row:

    Begin installation from the bottom, working your way up. Ensure the first row is level.

  6. Fastening:

    Use corrosion-resistant nails or screws. Fasten 3/4" from the top edge of the plank at each stud. Do not overdrive fasteners.

  7. Continue Installation:

    Install subsequent rows, staggering the joints for a more appealing look. Maintain a 1/4" gap at all openings and stops for caulking.

  8. Finishing Touches:

    Install trim boards around windows, doors, and corners. Caulk all gaps and joints with a high-quality exterior grade caulk.

  9. Painting:

    If planks are not pre-finished, paint within 180 days of installation using 100% acrylic exterior grade paint.

Pro Tip: Always refer to the manufacturer's installation guide for specific details and any updates to installation procedures.

Maintenance:

Regularly inspect and clean your Hardie® VL Plank siding. Repaint as needed, typically every 7-15 years depending on your climate and exposure.

Conclusion:

Proper installation of Hardie® VL Plank will ensure long-lasting, beautiful results. Always prioritize safety and follow manufacturer guidelines for the best outcome.
