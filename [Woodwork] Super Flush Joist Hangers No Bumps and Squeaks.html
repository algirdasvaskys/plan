Super Flush Joist Hangers: Achieve a Bump-Free, Squeak-Free Floor

Super Flush Joist Hangers are an excellent solution for creating a stable, quiet floor without bumps or squeaks. Follow this comprehensive guide to install them correctly.

Materials Needed:

  • Super Flush Joist Hangers
  • Joists
  • Rim joist or ledger board
  • Structural screws
  • Nails (as specified by hanger manufacturer)
  • Level
  • Tape measure
  • Pencil
  • Power drill
  • Hammer or nail gun

Installation Process:

Step 1: Prepare the Rim Joist or Ledger Board

Ensure your rim joist or ledger board is level and securely fastened to the support structure.

Step 2: Mark Joist Locations

Measure and mark the precise locations for your joists on the rim joist or ledger board. Typically, joists are spaced 16" or 24" on center, but always check local building codes.

Step 3: Attach the Hangers

Position the Super Flush Joist Hangers at your marked locations. Ensure they are level and flush with the top of the rim joist or ledger board. Fasten the hangers using the manufacturer-recommended nails or screws.

Step 4: Install the Joists

Cut your joists to the correct length. Slide each joist into its corresponding hanger, ensuring it sits snugly and flush with the top of the hanger.

Step 5: Secure the Joists

Use structural screws or nails (as specified by the hanger manufacturer) to fasten the joists to the hangers. Ensure you use the correct fastener size and type.

Step 6: Double-Check Alignment

Verify that all joists are level and flush with the top of the rim joist or ledger board. Make any necessary adjustments.

Pro Tips:

  • Always follow the manufacturer's instructions for your specific Super Flush Joist Hangers.
  • Use a level frequently throughout the installation process to ensure everything remains straight and true.
  • Consider using construction adhesive between the joists and the hangers for added stability and squeak prevention.
  • If cutting pressure-treated lumber, apply an end-cut preservative to the cut surfaces to prevent decay.

By following these steps and using Super Flush Joist Hangers, you'll create a sturdy floor structure that minimizes the risk of bumps and squeaks, ensuring a high-quality, long-lasting result.
