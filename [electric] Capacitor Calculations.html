Capacitor Calculations

Capacitors in Series

When capacitors are connected in series, the total capacitance is less than any individual capacitance. The formula for calculating the total capacitance of capacitors in series is:

1/Ctotal = 1/C1 + 1/C2 + 1/C3 + ...

Where Ctotal is the total capacitance, and C1, C2, C3, etc., are the individual capacitances.

Capacitors in Parallel

When capacitors are connected in parallel, the total capacitance is the sum of all individual capacitances. The formula for calculating the total capacitance of capacitors in parallel is:

Ctotal = C1 + C2 + C3 + ...

Where Ctotal is the total capacitance, and C1, C2, C3, etc., are the individual capacitances.
