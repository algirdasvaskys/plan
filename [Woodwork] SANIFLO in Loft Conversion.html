Using SANIFLO in a Loft Conversion

SANIFLO systems are an excellent solution for adding plumbing to loft conversions where traditional gravity-fed drainage is impractical. This guide will walk you through the process of installing a SANIFLO system in your loft conversion.

What is SANIFLO?

SANIFLO is a macerating pump system that allows you to install toilets, sinks, showers, and other plumbing fixtures in locations where conventional plumbing is not possible or too costly.

Why Use SANIFLO in a Loft Conversion?

  • Eliminates the need for extensive structural modifications
  • Allows for flexible placement of bathroom fixtures
  • Cost-effective compared to traditional plumbing solutions
  • Quick and easy installation

Steps to Install SANIFLO in a Loft Conversion

Step 1: Planning and Assessment

Evaluate your loft space and determine the best location for your new bathroom fixtures and the SANIFLO unit. Ensure you have access to electricity and a way to connect to the existing soil stack.

Step 2: Choose the Right SANIFLO Model

Select a SANIFLO model that suits your needs. For a complete bathroom, consider models like SANIFLO Sanibest Pro or SANIFLO Sanipack Pro UP.

Step 3: Install the SANIFLO Unit

Place the SANIFLO unit behind the toilet or in a concealed location. Ensure it's easily accessible for maintenance.

Step 4: Connect the Toilet

Install the toilet and connect it directly to the SANIFLO unit using the provided connectors.

Step 5: Install Other Fixtures

Install other bathroom fixtures (sink, shower, etc.) and connect them to the SANIFLO unit following the manufacturer's instructions.

Step 6: Install Discharge Pipe

Run a small-diameter (usually 22mm or 32mm) discharge pipe from the SANIFLO unit to the soil stack. This pipe can be run horizontally or vertically as needed.

Step 7: Electrical Connection

Connect the SANIFLO unit to a proper electrical supply. This should be done by a qualified electrician.

Step 8: Testing

Once everything is connected, test the system thoroughly to ensure proper function and check for any leaks.

Important Considerations

  • Always follow local building codes and regulations
  • Ensure proper ventilation in the loft bathroom
  • Consider soundproofing to minimize noise from the SANIFLO unit
  • Regular maintenance is crucial for the longevity of the system

Warning

While SANIFLO installation can be a DIY project, it's recommended to hire a professional plumber, especially for loft conversions where proper installation is crucial to prevent leaks and damage to the floors below.

Conclusion

Using a SANIFLO system in your loft conversion can be an effective way to add a bathroom without major structural changes. With proper planning and installation, you can enjoy a fully functional bathroom in your newly converted loft space.
