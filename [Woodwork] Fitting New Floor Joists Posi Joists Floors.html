The Big Build 17: Fitting New Floor Joists

Posi Joists Floors: An Introduction

Posi Joists, also known as metal web joists, are an innovative and efficient solution for floor construction. They combine the strength of timber with the versatility of metal to create a robust and flexible flooring system. This guide will walk you through the process of fitting new floor joists using Posi Joists.

Method for Fitting Posi Joists

Step 1: Preparation

Ensure the building is structurally sound and ready for new joists. Clear the area and check that supporting walls are level and plumb.

Step 2: Measuring and Ordering

Measure the span and calculate the required joist size and spacing. Order Posi Joists according to your specifications.

Step 3: Installing Joist Hangers

Attach joist hangers to the supporting walls at the correct height and spacing.

Step 4: Positioning the Joists

Lift the Posi Joists into place, fitting them securely into the joist hangers.

Step 5: Securing the Joists

Nail or screw the joists to the hangers according to manufacturer specifications.

Step 6: Installing Blocking

Add blocking between joists at mid-span and ends to increase lateral stability.

Step 7: Running Services

Utilize the open web design to run electrical, plumbing, and HVAC services through the joists.

Step 8: Adding Insulation

Install insulation between the joists if required for thermal or acoustic purposes.

Step 9: Laying the Subfloor

Install the subfloor material (typically plywood or OSB) over the joists, ensuring it's properly secured.

Pro Tip:

Always follow the manufacturer's guidelines and local building codes when installing Posi Joists. Proper installation is crucial for the structural integrity and longevity of your floor.

Conclusion

Posi Joists offer a versatile and efficient solution for modern floor construction. Their unique design allows for easy installation of services and provides excellent structural performance. By following these steps and adhering to best practices, you can create a robust and flexible flooring system that will stand the test of time.
