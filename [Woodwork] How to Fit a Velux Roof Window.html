How to Fit a Velux Roof Window

Safety First

Before starting, ensure you have the necessary safety equipment and are comfortable working at height. If you're unsure, consult a professional.

Step 1: Preparation

  • Measure the area where you want to install the window
  • Purchase the correct size Velux window and flashing kit
  • Gather necessary tools: saw, hammer, drill, level, tape measure, screwdriver

Step 2: Create the Opening

  • Mark the area on the roof where the window will be installed
  • Remove roof tiles or slates from the marked area
  • Cut through battens and felt, creating an opening slightly larger than the window

Step 3: Install Support Battens

  • Cut and fit support battens around the opening
  • Ensure the battens are level and securely fastened

Step 4: Fit the Window

  • Place the window into the opening from the outside
  • Secure the window using the brackets provided
  • Check that the window is level and adjust if necessary

Step 5: Install Flashing

  • Follow the manufacturer's instructions to install the flashing kit
  • Ensure all parts of the flashing are correctly overlapped for waterproofing

Step 6: Insulate and Finish

  • Insulate around the window frame
  • Replace any removed roof tiles or slates
  • Trim any excess felt or insulation

Step 7: Interior Finishing

  • Install the interior lining kit if provided
  • Paint or varnish any exposed wood to match your interior

Final Checks

Test the window to ensure it opens and closes smoothly. Check for any leaks during rainfall. If you encounter any issues or are unsure at any point, consult a professional Velux installer.
