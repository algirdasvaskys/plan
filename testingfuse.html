Troubleshooting Tips for Standard Fuse (Good)

If the measured resistance is not 0Ω (Continuity), check for:

  • Loose connections in the fuse holder.
  • Corrosion or oxidation on the terminals.
  • Ensure the fuse is properly seated in its holder.

Troubleshooting Tips for Standard Fuse (Blown)

If the measured value is not OL (Open Line), check for:

  • Short circuits downstream of the fuse.
  • Overcurrent conditions that caused the fuse to blow.
  • Correct fuse rating for the circuit's load.

Note: Replace with a fuse of the correct rating after addressing the root cause.
