It Works by RHJ

Summary and Themes

Summary

"It Works" is a concise guide that outlines a simple formula for achieving personal success. The book emphasizes the power of focused thought and the importance of writing down clear goals. RHJ presents a method that involves believing in the desired outcome, repeatedly visualizing success, and acting with faith and persistence.

Themes

  • Power of Belief: The book stresses that belief is a key factor in achieving goals.
  • Visualization: Visualizing success helps to manifest it in reality.
  • Goal Setting: Writing down specific goals is crucial to clarify and focus efforts.
  • Persistence: Consistent effort and faith are necessary to overcome obstacles and achieve success.
