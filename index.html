Acceleration

Physics a = Δv / Δt (m/s²) Key concept
Rate of change of velocity with time

Acceleration is the rate of change in velocity with time.

Key points

  • Formula: a = Δv / Δt, units m/s².
  • Vector quantity (magnitude and direction).
