16 Amp Socket Outlet Circuit Testing Guide

1. Continuity of CPC (Circuit Protective Conductor)

Ensure circuit is isolated before testing!
  • Disconnect circuit from supply
  • Short out Line and CPC at distribution board
  • Test resistance between Line and CPC at socket
  • Expected reading: < 1Ω
Maximum acceptable resistance: R₂ = (L × ρ × 2) / A where L = length, ρ = resistivity, A = cross-sectional area

2. Polarity Testing

  • Check Line conductor is connected to correct terminal
  • Verify Neutral conductor is connected to correct terminal
  • Confirm Earth conductor is connected to Earth terminal
  • Test socket with socket tester

3. Insulation Resistance

Use 500V DC test voltage for 230V circuits
  • Test between Line and Neutral
  • Test between Line and Earth
  • Test between Neutral and Earth
  • Minimum acceptable reading: 1MΩ
Expected readings:
L-N: >2MΩ
L-E: >2MΩ
N-E: >2MΩ
