PAS 63100-2024: New Battery System Standards

Important Notice: As of December 19, 2024, new regulations prohibit the installation of battery systems in loft spaces.

Key Changes

  • Complete ban on battery storage systems in residential loft spaces
  • Enhanced fire safety requirements for existing installations
  • New ventilation requirements for battery storage areas
  • Mandatory professional installation and certification
  • Regular inspection requirements for existing systems
October 2024: PAS 63100-2024 announced
December 19, 2024: Standards come into effect
June 2025: Deadline for existing installation compliance

Required Actions

  • Existing loft installations must be relocated by June 2025
  • New installations must comply with ground-floor or basement location requirements
  • Professional assessment required for current installations
  • Updated safety certificates must be obtained
