Warren Buffett's Wisdom

Big Opportunities Never Last Long

"Opportunities come infrequently. When it rains gold, put out the bucket, not the thimble."

Key Principle #1: Quick Decision Making

Buffett is known for making billion-dollar decisions within days or even hours when big opportunities present themselves.

Key Principle #2: Preparation is Essential

Success comes from being prepared when opportunities arise. Study markets, companies, and trends continuously.

Key Principle #3: Size Matters

When you find a great opportunity, invest substantially. Small investments in great opportunities won't create significant wealth.

Be Fearful When Others Are Greedy

Market downturns often present the biggest opportunities.

Circle of Competence

Only invest in what you truly understand.

Margin of Safety

Always buy with a significant margin of safety.
