How to Fit Velux Blinds: A Super Easy Guide

Fitting Velux blinds is a straightforward process that you can easily do yourself. Follow these simple steps to install your new blinds quickly and efficiently.

What You'll Need:

  • Your Velux blind
  • A screwdriver
  • A ladder (if necessary)

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Prepare Your Window

Ensure your Velux window is clean and dry. Open the window fully to give yourself easy access to the frame.

Step 2: Locate the Brackets

Look for the pre-drilled holes or existing brackets at the top of your Velux window frame. These are where you'll attach your blind.

Step 3: Attach the Brackets

If your window doesn't have pre-installed brackets, screw the provided brackets into the pre-drilled holes at the top of the window frame.

Step 4: Prepare the Blind

Unpack your Velux blind. It should come with two side rails and the main blind unit.

Step 5: Install the Side Rails

Clip the side rails into place on either side of the window frame. They should slot in easily.

Step 6: Attach the Blind

Hold the main blind unit up to the top of the window. The top of the blind should click into the brackets you installed or that were already present.

Step 7: Secure the Blind

Once the top is in place, gently pull the blind down and guide the sides into the side rails you installed earlier.

Step 8: Test the Blind

Carefully raise and lower the blind to ensure it moves smoothly and stays in place when raised.

Tips:

  • Always refer to the specific instructions that come with your Velux blind, as there may be slight variations depending on the model.
  • If you're uncomfortable working at heights, consider hiring a professional to install your blind.
  • Keep your receipt and packaging until you're sure the blind fits and functions correctly.

That's it! You've successfully installed your Velux blind. Enjoy your new window covering and the improved light control in your room.
