Introduction

Power factor correction using capacitors can help reduce electricity bills by improving the power factor of an electrical system. This document explains how to calculate the appropriate capacitor value for power factor correction.

Why Power Factor Correction?

A low power factor increases the apparent power drawn from the utility, resulting in higher electricity bills. By improving the power factor, you can reduce the apparent power and potentially save on your electricity costs.
