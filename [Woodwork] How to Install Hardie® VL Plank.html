How to Install Hardie速 VL Plank

Warning: Always follow local building codes and manufacturer's instructions. Wear appropriate safety gear, including eye protection, dust mask, and gloves.

Step 1: Preparation

  • Ensure the wall substrate is clean, dry, and properly flashed.
  • Install a weather-resistant barrier (WRB) over the substrate.
  • Install furring strips or a rainscreen system if required by local codes or for additional moisture management.

Step 2: Install Starter Strip

  • Install a starter strip at the bottom of the wall, ensuring it's level.
  • The starter strip should be at least 6 inches above grade for siding that starts at ground level.

Step 3: Install Trim

  • Install HardieTrim速 boards around windows, doors, and other openings.
  • Leave a 1/4 inch gap between the trim and the siding for caulking.

Step 4: Begin Siding Installation

  • Start at the bottom of the wall and work your way up.
  • Snap a level chalk line to ensure the first course is straight.
  • Place the first plank into the starter strip, ensuring it's level.

Step 5: Secure the Planks

  • Use 2-inch galvanized or stainless steel nails.
  • Nail through the nailing line at the top of each plank.
  • Space nails 16 inches apart on center, and at least 3/4 inch from plank ends.
  • Do not overdrive nails. They should be flush with the plank surface.

Step 6: Continue Installation

  • Install subsequent courses, ensuring each plank locks securely into the one below.
  • Stagger the butt joints of the siding planks so they don't align on adjacent courses.
  • Maintain a minimum 1 1/4 inch overlap between courses.

Step 7: Cut and Fit Around Openings

  • Use a circular saw with a fine-toothed blade to cut planks as needed.
  • For small cuts or intricate shapes, use a jigsaw or fiber cement shears.
  • Always cut fiber cement outdoors and wear a dust mask.

Step 8: Finishing Touches

  • Caulk around windows, doors, and other penetrations.
  • Touch up any nail heads or small damages with color-matched touch-up paint.
  • Clean up any debris and dust from the installation process.
Note: This guide provides general instructions. Always refer to the specific Hardie速 VL Plank installation guide and local building codes for detailed requirements and best practices.
