How to Hang a Door in 10 Minutes

Tools and Materials Needed:

  • Door
  • Door hinges
  • Screwdriver or drill
  • Screws
  • Pencil
  • Level
  • Shims
  • Hammer

Step 1: Prepare the Door Frame (2 minutes)

Ensure the door frame is level and plumb. Use shims if necessary to adjust the frame.

Step 2: Attach the Hinges to the Door (3 minutes)

Mark the hinge positions on the door. Screw the hinges onto the door, ensuring they're flush and straight.

Tip: Pre-drill holes to prevent wood splitting.

Step 3: Position the Door (1 minute)

Place the door in the frame, using shims to create an even gap around all sides.

Step 4: Mark Hinge Positions on the Frame (1 minute)

With the door in position, mark where the hinges meet the frame.

Step 5: Attach Hinges to the Frame (2 minutes)

Remove the door. Screw the hinges to the frame at the marked positions.

Step 6: Hang the Door (1 minute)

Lift the door and align the hinges. Insert the hinge pins to connect the door to the frame.

Final Check and Adjustments

Test the door's operation. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure smooth opening and closing.

Tip: If the door doesn't close properly, slight adjustments to the hinge screws can help align it correctly.

Total Time: 10 minutes

Remember, practice makes perfect. Your first attempt might take longer, but with experience, you'll be able to hang a door quickly and efficiently.
