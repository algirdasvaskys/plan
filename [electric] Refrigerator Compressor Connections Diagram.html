Refrigerator Compressor Connections Diagram

Legend:

C - Common Terminal

R - Run Terminal

S - Start Terminal

Connection Method:

Step 1: Identify the Terminals

Locate the three terminals on the compressor: Common (C), Run (R), and Start (S).

Step 2: Connect the Starting Capacitor

Connect one lead of the starting capacitor to the Start (S) terminal and the other lead to the Run (R) terminal.

Step 3: Connect the Running Capacitor

Connect one lead of the running capacitor to the Common (C) terminal and the other lead to the Run (R) terminal.

Step 4: Power Supply Connection

Connect the power supply to the Common (C) and Run (R) terminals.

Step 5: Grounding

Ensure that the compressor body is properly grounded for safety.

Step 6: Final Check

Double-check all connections to ensure they are secure and correct before powering on the system.

Note: Always refer to the manufacturer's specifications and guidelines for the specific compressor model you are working with. Safety should be your top priority when working with electrical components.
